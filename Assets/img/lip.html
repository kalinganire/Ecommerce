    Red Lipstick Shade 1

    A showstopper red that will never go out of style - Sapphire Red lipstick is your weapon of choice if you're ready to make a statement wherever you go and a bold one at that!

     

    If you've ever lusted for a "one-coat wonder" product for your lips, your search ends here. Get a full-blooded burst of pigment with just a single swipe of the Sapphire Liquid Lipstick that will stay the day and right up till the night! Wine, coffee & date-proof - this is a matte lipstick that is guaranteed to last longer than your longest day. Available in 30 stunning shades that will make it difficult to choose - the question is, are you ready to commit to a lipstick that is built to last?

     

    How to Apply: For precise application, apply it to your bottom lip first and then tend to your upper lip. Since the formula is very pigmented, we suggest applying the colour to the bottom lip first, pressing your lips together and then filling in your Cupid’s bow.

     

    Benefits: The Sapphire Liquid Lipstick gives you opaque pigmentation with a single swipe that stays all day with zero feathering or fading. Our favourite mattifying fix contains a generous helping of Vitamin E and comes in a full range of 30 gorgeous shades that is guaranteed to make your OOTDs stand out.

     

    Additional Details: The Sapphire Liquid Lipstick is available in 10 gorgeous shades. This product is dermatologically tested & approved and 100% safe for your skin.

     

    Size: 10.5 ml.

     

    List of Ingredients: Isododecane, Dimethicone, Isododecane & Trimethylsiloxysilicate, Cyclopentasiloxane & Trimethylsiloxysilicate, Disteardimonium Hectorite, Propylene Carbonate, Vitamin – E, Propyl Paraben.
